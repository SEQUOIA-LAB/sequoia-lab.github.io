Welcome to the Sequoia Lab at the University of California, Merced. We focus on the intersection of computer security, quantum computing, and machine learning systems.

About Me
======
I am an Assistant Professor at the University of California, Merced.

Research Interests
======
* Post-quantum Cryptos and hardware-intrinsic security primitives (i.e., PUF, NTT and FHE)
* Quantum computer emulation and simulation system
* Machine learning systems and security analysis
* Cyber-physical system and autonomous driving systems
