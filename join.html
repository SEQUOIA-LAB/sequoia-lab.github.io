Open Positions for Fall 2026

We are actively recruiting two Ph.D. students (starting Fall 2026) and multiple Research Assistants (starting anytime). If you are passionate about embedded AI, cyber-physical systems, and edge computing, we would love to hear from you.

school Prospective Ph.D. Students

I am looking for self-motivated Ph.D. students who are interested in Embedded Systems, Edge AI, and Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS). Prior experience in hardware design, embedded programming, or machine learning frameworks is highly preferred but not strictly required if you are a quick learner.

Successful candidates will receive full financial support (tuition coverage + stipend).

How to Apply

  1. Check my Research and Publications pages to see if your interests match the lab's direction.
  2. Send me an email with the subject line "[Prospective PhD] Your Name - University".
  3. Attach your CV and transcripts.
  4. Briefly describe your research background and why you want to join YuCCA Lab.
info
Note: Due to the high volume of emails, I may not be able to reply to every inquiry. If you do not hear back within 2 weeks, please feel free to send a reminder or assume that there isn't a good match at this time. However, I read every email carefully.

menu_book Undergraduate Researchers

UC Merced undergraduates are welcome to join the lab for research experience. We usually have projects involving PCB design, 3D printing, drone assembly, and embedded coding (Python/C++).

  • Minimum commitment of 10 hours/week is expected.
  • Prior coursework in embedded systems or machine learning is a plus.
  • Opportunities to co-author papers and attend conferences.

Please email me your CV and unofficial transcript with the subject "[Undergrad Research] Your Name".

flight_takeoff Visiting Students & Scholars

We welcome visiting students and scholars who have their own funding. We can provide office space, computing resources, and research mentorship. The duration is typically 6 months to 1 year.
